Project Scope

Bloom Workspace was an original concept that focused on developing a platform where people can create and discover collaborative work spaces in city-areas such as Philly and NYC. Users can book online spaces for either themselves or their business/team, leading to further improve the commuter life as well. The learning objective of the project was to practice my visual and prototyping skills in Sketch by going through the low-mid-hi-fidelity phases.
